Project Description

Have you ever wanted to take advantage of ambitious young dreamers looking to make a name for themselves in a fantasy setting? Well now you can! Buy beer! Buy food! Buy people! Take your mercenaries on a merry trip robbing merchants and fighting off bandits all in the name of profit. There is no better time than now to become a venture capitalist.

User Guide

Preparing for the Trip:

  • '1', '2', '3' to buy mercenary
  • 'f' to buy food
  • 'b' to buy beer
  • 'g' to show/hide team information
  • 'x' to go out on an adventure!
On the Road:
  • '1', '2', '3', '4' to interact with events
  • 'g' to show/hide team information
  • 'o' to speed up
  • 'p' to pause
