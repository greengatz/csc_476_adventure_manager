User Guide

Preparing for the Trip:

  • '1', '2', '3' to buy mercenary
  • 'f' to buy food
  • 'b' to buy beer
  • 'x' to go out on an adventure!
On the Road:
  • '1', '2', '3', '4' to interact with events
  • 'g' to show/hide team information
  • 'IDK THIS KEY' to speed up
  • 'IDK THIS KEY' to pause
